Shadenets
and insect nets

Our shade nets are designed to protect crops, livestock, and people from the harsh effects of the sun, while our insect nets provide protection against insects and other pests. Our products are used in a variety of applications, including agriculture, horticulture, construction, and general outdoor use. We have a strong reputation for quality and customer service in the industry.

Water
Harvesting

Different locations have different pumping needs including centrifugal pumps for industries, water supply, waste water, and dosing to circulator pumps for heating and air conditioning.Ago ventures contractors build, test, and service pumps. With our extensive spares inventory, we can repair any pump our client buys from us.

Irrigation
Systems

We supply and installs irrigation systems,fittings and other requirements such as filters, pumps, and tanks. When our clients are not sure of an irrigation system to use, we ask them to bring a map of their property and we design, supply, and install an irrigation system. We offer services to both small-scale and commercial farms.
